What is DiscoverBlockchain?

DiscoverBlockchain aims to create a sufficiently flexible ecosystem that will serve both (1) the theoretic expansion of Blockchain knowledge and (2) the applied development of Blockchain initiatives.

● For academics, both learners and contributors to the distributed ledger mindspace, DiscoverBlockchain provides a platform for publication, peer-review, education and experimentation.

● For entrepreneurs and contractors looking to contract a service or build a team, DiscoverBlockchain’s job board employs the new standards of security and global payment enabled by cryptocurrency.

Then, beyond the conventional functions of the platform, we apply the transparent and objective DiscoverSCORE validation algorithm: DiscoverBlockchain’s chief differentiating value and unique application of Blockchain technology.

Fixing the Engine of Innovation

Like an untwining strand of DNA, academia and the workplace are spiraling away from each other, resulting in overspecialized academics, undertrained employees, tremendous waste, and stunted growth in traditional industries.

The forward advancement of distributed ledger technology, in principle, relies on the same harmony of its own growth. DiscoverBlockchain aims to quickly bring these twin aspects back into synergistic operation.

×

Flexible Education for Disruptive Conditions

As universities are challenged to adapt their content to the new generation, that very same generation already understands what an agile learning environment would look like.

New models must be student-centric, open-source, choose-your-own-adventure style education, with nuanced guidelines ensuring arrival at the best ending: a mix of general savvy with targeted competencies.

×

The Campus

The DiscoverBlockchain campus is a virtual platform which is a combination of a professional marketplace and think-tank that will house 2nd and 3rd generation Blockchain Technology projects and beyond.

Students can succeed as contractors, contractors can contribute as teachers, teachers can build as entrepreneurs, and entrepreneurs can learn as students - developing their history and network all the while.

×

Principles for Intuiting Competency

While enthusiasm for disruption and self-granted job titles are often necessary boxes for prospects to tick, there are several disparate qualifiers which, though intuitively valuable, are hard to demonstrate.

Identifying these factors and standardizing them can help us meet the need for at-a-glance methods for proving (or vetting) competency - without 3 month probations.

×

Universal Tenure

Qualification is not just what you know. Or who you know. Qualification is a constantly-updating consensus about you that is made by a decentralized, disparate whole.

An individual’s relative involvement with, or impact on, the global development of distributed ledger technology is approximated algorithmically, and given a DiscoverScore (DSCORE). DSCORE is a moving aggregate of an individual's diverse experiences, known collaborators, and gained reputation.
